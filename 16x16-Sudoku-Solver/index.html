How to play Sudoku

The goal of Sudoku is to fill in the 16x16 grid with numbers and letters so that each row, column, and 4x4 box contain the numbers between '0' to '9' and letters 'A' to 'F'. At the start of the game, the 16x16 grid will have some cells filled in, known as setters. You may not erase or modify any of the setters. Your task is to completely fill the grid with missing numbers and letters. letters

  • Any row must contain a unique number and letter
  • Any column must contain a unique number and letter
  • Any 4x4 box must contain a unique number and letter
